How to Use the Portfolio · Step by Step
Opening a Paper Trade
1. Go to Stock Research → search your instrument → click ▶ Run 12-Agent Analysis
2. Read the Portfolio Manager verdict in Trading Agents → Decision tab
3. Only trade if verdict is BUY or SELL with Medium/High conviction
4. Use the Position Sizing Calculator above to calculate shares
5. Go to Orders · Paper Trading panel → fill in Symbol, Market, Direction, Shares, Entry, Stop, Target → Submit
6. Your trade appears here immediately, logged forever in the database
Daily Monitoring Routine
Morning (10:00 EAT): Open Live Brief → Refresh → read macro + watchlist
Then: Run CIO Scan → read decision memo on all positions
Check: Are any open positions near stop-loss or take-profit?
Stop hit: Close the trade immediately. No exceptions.
Target hit: Close and take profit. Log the win.
Weekly: Re-run agents on every open position. If verdict flips → exit.
Position Sizing Rules
Max per position: 10% of fund
Max risk per trade: 2% of fund
Max open positions: 8 at once
Formula: Shares = (Fund × 2%) ÷ (Entry − Stop)
Example: Fund TZS 10M · Entry 2,800 · Stop 2,660 → Risk per share = 140 → Max shares = 200,000 ÷ 140 = 1,428 → Cap at 10% = 357 shares
When to Go Real
✅ 30+ paper trades completed
✅ Win rate above 50%
✅ Avg win ≥ 2× avg loss
✅ Every trade hit stop or target — no exceptions broken
✅ Real capital allocated to Resolut Asset Management

Feature Guide · How To Use Resolut Terminal
🧠 Analyst · Morning Brief
Opens every day with an AI-generated market brief covering DSE, NSE Kenya and NYSE. Use the prompt templates below the brief for one-click analysis — LP reports, risk reviews, stock decisions. Type any question in the chat to get a final buy/hold/sell decision with entry, target, stop-loss and confidence.
✅ Decisions · Execution Board
Shows final BUY / HOLD / TRIM decisions for every major position. Each card shows: signal, confidence %, entry price, target price, stop-loss, bulls argument, bears argument. Click any card to open the stock detail drawer with fundamentals and quick order entry.
💼 Portfolio · P&L Tracker
Shows all your live positions across DSE, NSE Kenya and NYSE. Today P&L, MTD P&L, YTD P&L, unrealised gains, targets and stop-losses all in one table. The allocation pie chart shows your exposure breakdown. The position sizing calculator tells you exactly how many shares to buy based on your risk tolerance.
🗺️ Heatmap · Market Overview
Visual colour map of all DSE and NSE stocks. Green = rising, Red = falling, Gold = flat. Instantly see which sectors are hot. Below the heatmap is the earnings calendar showing upcoming results dates and EPS estimates for your holdings.
🇹🇿 DSE · Tanzania Market
Full table of all 20 DSE-listed stocks. Shows price (TZS), 1-day change, YTD performance, volume, sector, estimated P/E ratio, RSI, and signal. Click any row to open the stock detail panel with fundamentals, 30-day chart, news, and quick buy/sell button. Data sourced from african-markets.com.
🇰🇪 NSE · Kenya Market
Full table of 15 NSE Kenya stocks including Safaricom, Equity Group, KCB, COOP and more. Same layout as DSE. Click any ticker to see full fundamentals. NSE recorded +34% NASI return in 2024 — one of Africa's best-performing exchanges. Data in KES.
🌍 NYSE · Global ETFs
Live prices from Alpha Vantage API for VOO, SPY, GLD and EEM. Refreshes every 60 seconds automatically. Shows USD price, change, volume, RSI, and signal. Also shows live USD/TZS exchange rate and Gold spot price. These are the only truly live-data tickers in the terminal.
🔍 Screener · Find Stocks
Filter stocks across all 3 markets using natural language or dropdowns. Examples: "DSE banks with momentum" · "NSE stocks YTD above 20%" · "All buy signals" · "Oversold RSI below 35". Use preset hint buttons for quick screens. Results show full detail — click any result to open stock drawer.
⚖️ Compare · Two Stocks
Type any two tickers and click Compare. Gets a side-by-side table showing YTD return, P/E ratio, ROE, dividend, 1-day change and sector. Also generates a bar chart comparison. Great for deciding between CRDB vs NMB, or SCOM vs EQTY before making a trade decision.
💵 Orders · Paper Trading
Simulated trading — place paper BUY or SELL orders across DSE, NSE Kenya, and NYSE without real money. Choose Market Order, Limit Order, or Stop Order. All orders logged with timestamp. Use this to test your strategy before committing real capital. When ready for real trading, connect IBKR for NYSE or Orbit Securities for DSE.
📈 Backtest · Strategy Testing
Test any of Resolut's 3 strategies on any stock before going live. Pick ticker, strategy (MA Crossover · RSI · Momentum), time period (30–365 days) and capital amount. Results show: total return %, win rate, max drawdown, Sharpe ratio. Equity curve chart shows performance over time. Run this before every new trade idea.
⚠️ Risk · Exposure Dashboard
Shows VaR (Value at Risk), max drawdown, Sharpe ratio, Sortino ratio, and beta vs SPY. Exposure table shows allocation % per market, FX risk, and stop-loss status. Risk alerts highlight liquidity issues, FX exposure, and overbought signals. NAV vs SPY chart shows if Resolut is outperforming the market.
🔔 Price Alerts · Notifications
Set price alerts for any ticker. Conditions: price above/below a level, RSI above/below a threshold, or MA crossover. All active alerts shown in a list. The bell icon in the sidebar opens the live alert feed showing the most recent signal triggers. Alerts are stored in the current session.
📁 Documents · Compliance
All Resolut's compliance documents, LP reports, trade logs, research memos and broker account records in one searchable library. Search by name or topic. Each document shows its status: ACTIVE, FILED, PENDING, DRAFT. LP report due April is PENDING — due May 1.
How To Add A New Feature Or Market
Everything in Resolut is in one HTML file. To add a new feature:

Add a new market (e.g. JSE South Africa, Egyptian Exchange): Tell me the market name and main tickers — I add the data array, sidebar icon, and full table in under 10 minutes.

Add a new instrument (e.g. Forex, Bonds, Crypto): Same process — I add a new panel with the relevant data fields and AI analysis context.

Add a new AI feature (e.g. automatic LP report generator): I extend the Claude AI system prompt and add a button. Takes about 5 minutes.

Current data limitations to know: DSE and NSE prices are end-of-day (updated once daily from african-markets.com). NYSE prices via Alpha Vantage free tier are 15-min delayed. For real-time DSE/NSE data, a paid data provider like Refinitiv or Bloomberg would be needed. News headlines are currently curated samples — a NewsAPI integration would make them live.

To make it fully live and production-grade: Host on Vercel or Netlify (free), add a backend for real-time DSE scraping, and connect IBKR API for real order execution. I can build each of these steps with you.
